Chicken Casserole

Description

Saucy chicken, bacon, Cheddar, and almonds are baked with a buttery cracker topping in this chicken casserole.

Ingredients

  • Cooked Chicken
  • Can Cream of Chicken Soup
  • Sour Cream
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Sliced Green Onions
  • Slivered Almonds
  • Bacon Slices
  • Hot Sauce
  • Buttery Crackers
  • Melted Butter

Steps

  1. Cook the macaroni in salted water.
  2. Mix together mayonnaise, mustard, seasonings, vinegar, and sugar to taste.
  3. Stir in the macaroni and remaining ingredients. Chill in the refrigerator.
