Macaroni Salad

Description

This macaroni salad is a classic dish perfect for picnics and barbecues. It's creamy, tangy, and loaded with vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Mayonnaise
  • White Sugar
  • White Vinegar
  • Salt
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Bell Pepper
  • Carrot
  • Pimento Peppers

Steps

  1. Cook the macaroni in salted water.
  2. Mix together mayonnaise, mustard, seasonings, vinegar, and sugar to taste.
  3. Stir in the macaroni and remaining ingredients. Chill in the refrigerator.
